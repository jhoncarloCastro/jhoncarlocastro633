About

I'm an automotive technician based in Calamba City, Philippines, with hands-on experience in vehicle maintenance, engine repair, chassis inspection, and electrical systems. My background combines vocational training in mechatronics with real-world work at an industrial facility.

Currently enrolled in TVET Automotive Technology at Don Bosco College Canlubang, I'm deepening my expertise in all aspects of automotive repair. I focus on doing the job right the first time — thorough inspection, accurate diagnosis, and clean repair work. Whether it's a scheduled PMS or a fault that needs tracking down, I approach every job with the same level of attention and care.
